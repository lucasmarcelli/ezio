Artist

Art is a window to the soul.

Ezio Marcelli studied Fine Art near his hometown of Chiaiamari, Italy in Frosinone. Ezio gave up his career as an Artist when he moved to Canada in order to better provide for his family in his new home.

His usual style of art is a stunning abstract representation of various forms. A single painting of his can take months depending on the size due to his precise methods that require significant planning and attention to detail. More recently he has expanded his medium in creating an amazing lifelike portrait of his wife Sandra.

Craftsman

Our hands bring our ideas to life.

Woodwork, masonry, painting, carpentry and more. Ezio excels in any projects that require working with your hands and has mastered the balance between precision and craftsmanship.

He has fixed, remodelled and improved every house his family has ever lived in. From finishing the basement to renovating the kitchen, there is nothing that Ezio can't make better. His eye for perfection is unparalleled, and his skill in getting it done is incredible.

Grandfather

No greater joy in life.

Despite all the work he does, Ezio Marcelli is a father and grandfather before anything else. He loves his family more than anything.

He has made countless sacrifices throughout his life in the interest of his family and children. He moved across the ocean to Canada without his wife or the ability to speak English. He gave up his art and worked and worked until his wife Sandra could join him so they could get their family to where it is today. He now has two sons and two grandchildren he adores and continues to make daily sacrifices for them. Neither of his children would be where they are today without him.
