Artist

Art is a window to the soul.

Ezio studied art in Frosinone, near where he grew up in Italy. The style of art is a stunning abstract representation of various forms. A single painting can take months depending on size because the method used requires extreme precsion and planning.

Ezio gave up art as a career when he moved to Canada in order to provide better for his family. Despite this, in recent years he has done a portrait of his wife Sandra and is still as talented as ever.

Craftsman

Our hands bring our ideas to life.

Woodwork, masonry, painting, carpentry and more, Ezio is very skilled with projects that require your hands.

Over the years, he has fixed, remodelled and improved many things in every house his family has ever lived in. From finishing the basement to renovating the kitchen, there is nothing that Ezio can't make better. His eye for what looks good is unparalelled, and his skill in getting it done is incredible.

Grandfather

No greater joy in life.

Ezio is a father and grandfather before anything else. He loves his family more than anything and turns into a complete softie when his grandchildren come around.

Many sacrifices were made in the interest of his family and children. He moved across the ocean without knowing english. He gave up his art. He didn't see his wife for a couple years after moving. Slowly but surely, he worked and worked (along with Sandra) and got his family to where it is today.
